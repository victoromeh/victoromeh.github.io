About

I'm an architectural designer with a Bachelor of Architecture and hands-on experience producing permit-ready drawing sets for residential and commercial projects. I work in Revit, AutoCAD, and SketchUp, and I'm comfortable in both imperial and metric units, so location is never a constraint.

My focus is on the technical production side of architecture — BIM modeling, construction documents, drawing set coordination, and 3D visualization — built around what actually gets a permit set approved.
